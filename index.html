// Parallels

Same Mentality.

01 — Process Optimization
Data in, improvement out.
Every training block starts with data: splits, power output, heart rate drift. Every sprint starts the same way — metrics, bottlenecks, iteration. I optimized my marathon pace by analyzing cadence data the same way I profiled API response times at Bosch.
02 — Resilience Under Load
Pressure is the constant.
Kilometer 35 in a marathon, hour 4 at the Ironman, production incident on a Friday evening. The ability to stay calm under pressure and work systematically — I train that in both worlds.
03 — Long-Term Commitment
Years, not sprints.
Sub-3 marathon takes 2+ years of consistent training. Becoming a Security Engineer takes just as long. I'm investing in both — not because it's quick, but because I understand the process.
04 — Feedback Loops
Honest self-assessment.
Strava data after every run, code reviews after every PR, match analysis after every table tennis game. Growth comes from honest evaluation, not comfortable assumptions.
